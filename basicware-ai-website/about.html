About BASICWARE AI

AI solutions with Hong Kong vision

Our Story

Founded in Hong Kong, BASICWARE AI represents the perfect fusion of Eastern innovation and Western business acumen. We leverage Hong Kong's unique position as a global financial hub and gateway to Asia to deliver cutting-edge AI solutions worldwide.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize artificial intelligence and make it accessible to enterprises and governments across the Asia-Pacific region. Today, we stand as a testament to Hong Kong's technological prowess and international connectivity.

From our strategic base in Hong Kong, we've expanded our reach across Southeast Asia, building bridges between advanced AI technologies and real-world business challenges. Our team combines deep technical expertise with cultural understanding, enabling us to deliver solutions that truly resonate with local markets while maintaining global standards.
